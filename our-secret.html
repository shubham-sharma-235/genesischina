Our Secret

Four Decades of Focus

At Genesis Fabrics, our story began over 40 years ago in Seoul, Korea, with a simple yet powerful mission: to solve our customers’ fabric sourcing and development challenges. We’ve grown into a global player in the textile industry, but our core philosophy remains the same — deliver quality, innovation, and reliability at every step. The secret to our success lies in three pillars: our dream, our team, and our theme.

Our Dream

Partner in Creativity

From the very beginning, we dreamed of becoming more than just a fabric supplier — a trusted partner who helps bring creative visions to life. Whether sourcing the perfect base or developing a custom textile from scratch, we turn challenges into opportunities.

In 2000, we opened our Shanghai and Shaoxing offices in China. This move empowered us to deliver high-quality fabrics made in China to global customers, combining local expertise with international standards. Today, these offices are our operational hubs — keeping us close to clients and their timelines.
