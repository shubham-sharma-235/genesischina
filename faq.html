No, we are not a factory. Instead, we partner with a network of high-quality, audited factories to bring you the best fabrics. This approach offers several key benefits:

  • Broader Range: By working with multiple factories, we can offer a wider variety of textiles to meet your specific needs.
  • Flexibility: If one factory faces delays or issues, we can quickly shift production to another trusted facility, ensuring your deadlines are met.
  • Specialization: Each factory we work with specializes in specific types of fabrics, ensuring expertise and quality in every product.
  • Cost Efficiency: Our partnerships allow us to negotiate better prices and pass the savings on to you.
  • Scalability: Whether you need a small batch or a large order, we can scale production seamlessly to match your requirements.

Absolutely! We are fully equipped to comply with your market-specific requirements and ensure that all fabrics meet the necessary test criteria for your market or brand. We offer:

  • GRS (Global Recycled Standard) for recycled polyester and nylon.
  • BCI (Better Cotton Initiative), OCS (Organic Content Standard), and GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard) for organic cotton.

Our team will work closely with you to ensure that every fabric not only meets the required certifications but also complies with all test criteria specific to your market or brand. Just communicate your needs, and we’ll provide the necessary documentation and support to ensure full compliance.

Yes, sustainability is a key focus for us. We offer a wide range of eco-friendly fabrics, including:
